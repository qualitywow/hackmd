## III. Interference Characterization and Outage
### A. Interference
$I(x) \triangleq \Sigma_{Y \in \Phi_t} l(||x-Y||)$
$\Phi_t$: point process of transmitters on $\mathbb{R}^2$

e.g. slotted ALOHA 設定的傳送機率為 $p$，那 $\Phi_t$就是一個強度為p的point process
